What is Velodrome Finance?

Velodrome Finance is a decentralized exchange (DEX) and liquidity protocol built on the Optimism layer-2 scaling solution for Ethereum. Velodrome Finance employs an automated market maker (AMM) model, allowing users to trade various cryptocurrencies directly from their wallets without the need for intermediaries.
